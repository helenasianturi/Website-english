English Grammar Essentials

Nouns

Nouns are words that represent a person, place, thing, or idea. Examples: cat, house, happiness, London.

We will cover common nouns, proper nouns, countable and uncountable nouns.

Adjectives

Adjectives describe or modify nouns or pronouns. Examples: beautiful, tall, pink, happy.

Learn about comparative and superlative adjectives.

Basic Tenses

Understand the Present Simple, Past Simple, and Future Simple tenses to talk about different times.

Example: I study English. (Present) / I studied English. (Past) / I will study English. (Future)

Sentence Structure

Learn the basic Subject-Verb-Object (SVO) structure of English sentences.

Example: She (Subject) reads (Verb) a book (Object).
