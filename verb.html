Mastering English Verbs

What are Verbs?

Verbs are action words or state of being words. They are essential in every sentence. Examples: run, eat, sleep, be, seem.

Common Regular Verbs

Regular verbs form their past tense and past participle by adding "-ed".

  • Play - Played - Played
  • Walk - Walked - Walked
  • Talk - Talked - Talked
  • Study - Studied - Studied

Common Irregular Verbs

Irregular verbs have unique past tense and past participle forms.

  • Go - Went - Gone
  • Eat - Ate - Eaten
  • See - Saw - Seen
  • Drink - Drank - Drunk

Verb "To Be"

One of the most important verbs. Present: am, is, are. Past: was, were.

Examples: I am happy. She was here. They are students.
